Year 5 Computing Lesson: Explore Scratch

Learning Objectives

  • Know the Scratch interface
  • Learn how to move sprites

Activities/Tasks

  1. Open your web browser and go to scratch.mit.edu.
  2. Click on "Sign In" and log into your Scratch account.
  3. Click on "Create" at the top of the page to open a new project.
  4. Observe the Scratch interface:
    • Stage area (top-right) where your sprite performs.
    • Blocks palette (left) containing code blocks.
    • Script area (center) where you drag blocks to code.
    • Sprite list (bottom-right) showing your sprites.
  5. Click on the Scratch Cat sprite to select it.
  6. From the "Motion" blocks category, drag out the block that says move 10 steps.
  7. Click the block to see the cat move forward.
  8. Add more blocks to make a simple animation:
    • Drag out turn 15 degrees and attach it to the "move" block.
    • Use repeat 10 from the "Control" section to repeat the movement.
  9. Click the green flag to run your code and watch your cat move.
  10. Try changing the number of steps or degrees to see different movements.

Resources

You'll need:

  • Computers
  • A Scratch account
