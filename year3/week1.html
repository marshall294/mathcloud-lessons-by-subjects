Year 3 Computing Lesson: Slide Design Basics

Learning Objectives

  • Understand basic slide layout
  • Learn about colours and contrast in design
  • Improve a slide by changing layout and colours

What is a Slide Presentation?

Slide Presentation: A group of slides you show to an audience. Each slide has text, pictures, or other content.

You use slide software like PowerPoint, Google Slides, or Keynote to create them.

Slide Design Tips

  • Keep it simple: Too much on a slide is confusing.
  • Use colours wisely: Make sure text is easy to read (light background + dark text).
  • Use large fonts: So everyone can read your slides.
  • Use pictures: Images help tell your story.

Activities/Tasks

  1. Open your computer and start your presentation software (PowerPoint, Google Slides, etc.).
  2. Create a new slide show with 3 slides.
  3. On slide 1, add a title: "My Favourite Animal" and your name.
  4. On slide 2, add a picture of your favourite animal and write 2 facts about it.
  5. On slide 3, create a bad design on purpose:
    • Use a dark background and dark text.
    • Use small font and too many words.
  6. Discuss as a class what makes the third slide hard to read.
  7. Now improve slide 3:
    • Change the background to a light colour.
    • Use bigger text and fewer words.
    • Add a picture that matches the topic.

Resources

  • Computers or laptops
  • Presentation software (e.g. PowerPoint or Google Slides)
