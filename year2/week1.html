Year 2 Computing Lesson: Introduction to Folders

Learning Objectives

  • Understand what folders are on a computer
  • Learn how to create and name a folder
  • Create a folder named "My Work"

What is a Folder?

Folder: A folder on a computer is like a real-life folder. It helps keep your files organised in one place so you can find them easily.

Just like you put your drawings or worksheets into a paper folder at school, you can put pictures, documents, and other files into a folder on a computer.

Activities/Tasks

  1. Look at a real folder and talk about what it does.
  2. Look at a folder on the computer and say what it does.
  3. Follow these steps to make a folder:
    • Go to the desktop (or any folder on your computer).
    • Right-click in a blank space.
    • Choose New > Folder.
    • A new folder will appear with the name highlighted.
    • Type: My Work
    • Press Enter on your keyboard.
  4. Open the folder and talk about what you could put inside (e.g., pictures, documents, school work).
  5. Try deleting the folder (optional), then make it again to practise.

Resources

  • Computers or laptops
  • Practice files (optional)
