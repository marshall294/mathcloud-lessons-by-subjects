Introduction to Basic Word Processing Skills

Class Topic: What is Word Processing?

Word processing means using a computer to type, change, and save words. You can write stories, make lists, and even add pictures using a word processor like Microsoft Word, Google Docs, or Pages.

Step 1: Opening a Word Processor

Let's open a word processor! Follow these simple steps:

  1. Find the Icon: Look for an icon with a "W" (for Microsoft Word) or "Docs" (for Google Docs) on your computer or tablet.
  2. Click the Icon: If you're on a computer, double-click the icon. On a tablet, just tap it.
  3. A Blank Page Opens: After clicking, a new blank page will appear on the screen‚Äîready for your words!
